Formerly known as FujiXerox, the company is a multinational group that combines western and Japanese management styles in its business operations. As the only MPS support group manager for the region, the full independant management control given has allowed me to learn and grow tremendously. American Express

First company that I joined as a fresh graduate way back in 2001 into the contact center industry. Amex remains a fond memory, a place that helped me built good leadership foundations and long lasting friendships. It is sincere in its pursuance of ethics and optimisation of performance. I remembered it as a place with mostly intelligent people and has an extremely low ratio of corporate charlatans. It remains successful to this day and continues to attract top talents effortlessly. An ivy league of companies.
